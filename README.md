# 🌱 Journey Into Science and Biodiversity – A Career Story

## 1. Background – Where I Grew Up 🏡

I was born in a small town in **Panna, Madhya Pradesh** and grew up around nearby cities like Satna and Rewa. My father had a moving job, so we were always packing up and ready to go. For high school, I moved to **Bhopal**, the capital city of Madhya Pradesh. My childhood was nourished by Budeli and Baghel culture, and my adolescence enjoyed the freedom of Bhopal.

I was always a **visual learner** 👀, enjoying practical lessons, labs, outdoors 🌳, and trips. But school learning was challenging: remembering everything 📚, doing calculations ➕➖, and reading in a straight line was stressful before exams.

It was clear for me to go for science 🔬, although the school administration thought it was nearly impossible. Fortunately, my biology teacher supported me when I had to convince the school why I did not want commerce. This taught me that **nothing is wrong with me, and I must fight for what I choose** 💪.

Apart from studies, I loved **art** 🎨 and **sports** ⚽ classes. My parents are both **PhDs** 🎓 in social and political science, and my family also includes a **neurologist uncle** 🧠.

### Things I Loved as an Individual:

- Love for nature 🌳🐦
- Curiosity to discover something new 🔍
